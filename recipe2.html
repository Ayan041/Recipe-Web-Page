Spicy Baked Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5-6 ripe tomatoes, chopped (or 1 can crushed tomatoes)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 eggs
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish
  • Optional: feta cheese or chili flakes

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and sautÃ© until translucent.
  3. Add bell pepper and garlic; cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Make small wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them.
  6. Cover the pan and cook until eggs are done to your liking.
  7. Garnish with parsley or basil and optional feta cheese.
  8. Serve hot with bread or pita.
