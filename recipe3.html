Chicken Kababs

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp yogurt (optional, for tenderness)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water)

Preparation Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, yogurt, lemon juice, paprika, cumin, garlic paste, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add chicken cubes to the marinade. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight).
  3. Thread the marinated chicken onto soaked wooden skewers.
  4. Preheat a grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the skewers for 12â€“15 minutes, turning occasionally, until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney, salad, or garlic sauce.
