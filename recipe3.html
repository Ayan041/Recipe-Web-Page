Strawberry Custard

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup custard powder
  • 1 cup chopped strawberries
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil the milk in a saucepan.
  2. Mix custard powder with little cold milk and stir well.
  3. Add the custard mix to boiling milk while stirring.
  4. Add sugar and cook until thick.
  5. Add vanilla essence and strawberries. Chill before serving.
