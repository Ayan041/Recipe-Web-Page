Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped lettuce (romaine, arugula, or baby spinach)
  • 1/2 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup diced avocado
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup raspberries
  • 1/4 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup peach or apple slices
  • 1/4 cup green grapes
  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts (cashews or almonds)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or lime
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash all vegetables and fruits thoroughly.
  2. Chop or slice ingredients as listed.
  3. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, cabbage, tomatoes, and other veggies.
  4. Top with berries, grapes, avocado, and nuts.
  5. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
  6. Toss gently and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve fresh and chilled.
