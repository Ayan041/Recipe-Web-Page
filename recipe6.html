Butter Bean Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 can butter beans, drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2-3 eggs
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Bread slices, for serving

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sautÃ© the onions and bell pepper until soft.
  2. Add garlic, paprika, and cumin; stir for a minute.
  3. Pour in chopped tomatoes and simmer for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Add butter beans, season with salt and pepper, and cook another 5 minutes.
  5. Make small wells and crack eggs into them.
  6. Cover and cook until eggs are set to your preference.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with crusty buttered bread.
